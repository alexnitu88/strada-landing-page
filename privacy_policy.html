Privacy Policy for Looks Max

Last Updated: February 14, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Looks Max. This privacy policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, protection, and sharing of your information. We are dedicated to safeguarding the privacy of our users and ensuring transparency in our data handling processes.

How Looks Max Uses Collected Face Data

We collect your face data exclusively to analyze your facial attractiveness. Our AI algorithms assess your facial features to provide you with a personalized attractiveness rating and recommendations for aesthetic enhancements. This data is used solely for this purpose and is not employed in any other manner.

Data Retention Policy

We retain your face data for a period of 30 days, after which it is automatically purged from our systems. During this period, the data is not utilized for any purpose other than providing you with our service.

Data Collection Methodology

Your face data is obtained directly from the photographs you upload to the app. These images undergo analysis by our proprietary algorithms, which evaluate your facial characteristics to generate an attractiveness rating and suggestions for improvement.

Data Disclosure and Sharing

Your face data is not shared, sold, or disclosed to third parties, except as required by law. All face data is securely stored within our database, which remains inaccessible to external parties.

Data Security Measures

To protect your face data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse, we implement state-of-the-art encryption methods and secure data storage technologies.

Your Rights and Choices

You possess the right to access, rectify, or delete your face data at any moment by contacting our customer support.

Additional Data Collection

  • Photographs: The photographs you upload are utilized for analysis and recommendation purposes.
  • Personal Information: Basic personal details such as age, gender, and location are collected to customize our recommendations.
  • Usage Data: We gather data on your interaction with our app, like feature usage and engagement time, to enhance our services.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Third-Party Sharing: We do not engage in selling or renting your personal data to others.

Data Security

We employ rigorous security protocols to safeguard your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Nevertheless, we acknowledge that no system can guarantee absolute security.

User Rights and Control

  • Access and Correction: You are entitled to access and update your personal information.
  • Deletion: You can request the removal of your account and related data.
  • Consent Withdrawal: Your consent to our data processing can be revoked at any time.

Policy Updates

We may periodically update this privacy policy. Any significant changes will be communicated to you, and the date of the most recent update will be noted.

Contact Information

Should you have any privacy-related questions or concerns, please reach out to us at team@ailooksmaxing.app.

Compliance with Laws

Our policy adheres to relevant data protection laws, including GDPR and CCPA. We are committed to resolving any issues concerning our collection or use of your personal information.

Effective Date

This policy takes effect as of February 14, 2024.
